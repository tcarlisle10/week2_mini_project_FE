Welcome to a little about Me

I grew up in a small town where being outdoors was a big part of my life. From a young age, I spent countless hours exploring the woods and learning to appreciate nature. As I got older, I became more involved in sports, playing baseball as a kid, then transitioning to football and track in middle school. I also started spending more time with friends, playing video games and enjoying the mix of outdoor activities and technology. After high school, I pursued my passion for hands-on work and became a welder. For 10 years, I dedicated myself to the craft, building a strong foundation in fabrication and metalwork.
